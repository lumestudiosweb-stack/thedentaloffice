About Our Practice

A Practice You Can Trust, in the Heart of Mangaluru

The Dental Office is a trusted, established, and affordable dental practice in Pumpwell, Mangaluru — serving patients of all ages from across the city and beyond. Co-founded by Dr. M S Prathap, M.D.S. (Endodontist & Aesthetic Dentist, 27 yrs) and Dr. Shruthy Prathap, M.D.S. (Periodontist & Implantologist), our clinic is built around a comfortable, welcoming environment, particularly for those who experience dental anxiety.

We offer same-day emergency appointments where possible and are committed to delivering high-quality, transparent dental care using the latest techniques and modern technology — without the jargon or unnecessary treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do Dr. M S Prathap and Dr. Shruthy Prathap practice?

Both Dr. M S Prathap, M.D.S. and Dr. Shruthy Prathap, M.D.S. practice at The Dental Office, located at 2nd Floor, Creek Galaxy, opp. Indiana Hospital Road, Pumpwell, Mangaluru, Karnataka 575002.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment directly through this website using the form below, or reach out via phone / WhatsApp at +91 78920 29550. Online booking through Practo is also available for in-clinic visits with the doctor.

What procedures are available at The Dental Office?

Dr. M S Prathap handles Root Canal Treatment (incl. microscope-assisted RCT), Ceramic Veneers and Crowns, Zirconia Crowns, Restorative, Cosmetic and Aesthetic Dentistry. Dr. Shruthy Prathap handles Periodontal (Gum) Treatment, Dental Implants, Bone Grafting, and Full-Arch Implant rehabilitation.

What are the doctors' qualifications?

Dr. M S Prathap holds a BDS from KVG Medical College, Sullia (1999) followed by an M.D.S. specialist qualification in Endodontics. Dr. Shruthy Prathap holds an M.D.S. specialist qualification in Periodontics and Implantology. Both doctors hold verified medical registrations.

What do the doctors specialise in?

Dr. M S Prathap, M.D.S. is an Endodontist and Aesthetic Dentist specialising in Root Canal, Restorative and Cosmetic Dentistry, with 27 years of overall experience (25 as a specialist). Dr. Shruthy Prathap, M.D.S. is a Periodontist and Implantologist specialising in Gum Diseases and Dental Implants — and is Co-Founder of The Dental Office.

What are the clinic's opening hours?

The doctors consult at The Dental Office on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday from 3:00 PM to 7:30 PM. Visits on other days are available by appointment — please call or WhatsApp +91 78920 29550 to arrange a time.

Is the clinic wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our clinic is wheelchair accessible. Please let us know in advance if you need any specific assistance and we'll be happy to help.
